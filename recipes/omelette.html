Omelette

Description

A quick recipe to make a light and fluffy ham and cheese omelette in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of vegetable oil
  • 4 slices deli ham, chopped into small pieces
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add ham and cook until browned and crispy, stirring frequently to avoid burning. (around 7 miunutes)
  2. Beat eggs with 1 teaspoon chives, salt, and pepper in a small bown before adding to skillet. Tilt skillet to distribute the egg evenly across the skillet.
  3. After the edges of the omelette is set, sprinkle cheddar cheese on top and fold the omelette in half
  4. Flip and cook until eggs is entirely set and the cheese inside is melted. (30 seconds to 1 minute)
  5. Slide onto plate and sprinkle with remaining chives
