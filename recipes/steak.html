Steak

Description

A simple steak prepared Argentinean style cooked in wine and rosemary

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 4 New York strip steaks, cut 1-inch thick

Steps

  1. Before cooking, combine red wine, salt, and rosemary in a small bowl. Let sit for 2-3 hours
  2. Allow steaks to sit for 30 minutes to an hour to reach room temperature before cooking
  3. Heat a large griddle or cast-iron skillet over high heat
  4. Once heated, place the steaks on hot pan and cook for 8 minutes per side or to desired doneness
  5. Pour in the wine mixture and allow to boil for a minute
  6. Serve steaks with sauce on a deep platter
