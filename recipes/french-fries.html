French Fries

Description

Golden crispy french fries that are easy to make with an air fryer

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • a Few teaspons of Vegetable oil
  • a Pinch of Cayenne Pepper and/or other spices such as garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika
  • Kosher Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into slices, then into sticks
  2. Soak potato sticks in cold water for 5 minutes then drain
  3. Put potato sticks into a bowl or pot, then pour boiling water over them. Let sit for 10 minutes then drain and dry potatos
  4. After potato sticks cool, transfer in a mixing bowl and mix them with spices and vegetable oil
  5. Air fry for 15 minutes, toss, then cook for 10 additional minutes
  6. Season with salt and serve
